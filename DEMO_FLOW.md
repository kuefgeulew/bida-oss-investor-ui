# Demo Flow

## DISCOVER AND DECIDE

### Which Tabs to Show
- OVERVIEW
- DISCOVER
- FDI INTEL
- INTELLIGENCE LAB
- ZONES

### Features to Highlight

OVERVIEW Tab:
- Click Next Best Action Panel to see journey guidance
- Show Quick Stats Grid with investment metrics
- Point out System Health Indicators (99.9% uptime badge)

DISCOVER Tab:
- Open SectorOpportunityHeatmap showing textile, pharma, IT sectors
- Click on sector card to view deep dive analysis
- Show CompetitorBenchmark comparing Bangladesh vs Vietnam, India
- Browse InvestmentOpportunityPipeline for curated deals

FDI INTEL Tab:
- Display FDIRealtimeDashboard showing national FDI inflows
- Point to FDISectorTrends chart showing sector performance
- Show FDISourceCountryMap with geographic visualization

INTELLIGENCE LAB Tab:
- Use IncentiveCalculator to project tax holiday savings
- Enter investment amount, show calculated incentive value
- Open FeeTransparencyCalculator to show all-in cost breakdown

ZONES Tab:
- Use ZoneRecommender AI to suggest optimal zone
- Open EnhancedZoneExplorer to browse Dhaka EPZ, Chittagong EPZ
- Show ZonePlotIntelligencePanel with plot availability

### What to Click
1. Login as Investor
2. Navigate OVERVIEW > DISCOVER > FDI INTEL > ZONES (left to right)
3. In DISCOVER, click textile sector card
4. In INTELLIGENCE LAB, enter 5000000 USD in IncentiveCalculator
5. In ZONES, click on Dhaka EPZ in zone explorer

### What NOT to Open
- Do not open Settings tab (contains admin-only features that are gated)
- Do not click expired notifications
- Skip compliance warnings if profile not complete

- IDENTITY
- BANKING
- SERVICES

### Features to Highlight

### What to Click
1. Navigate to IDENTITY tab
2. View BBIDCard
3. Navigate to BANKING tab
4. Click BankSelector, choose BRAC Bank
5. Navigate to SERVICES tab
6. Open RJSCRegistrationForm
